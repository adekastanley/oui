About

At the School of Entrepreneurship at Oduduwa University, we believe in nurturing innovative minds and turning ideas into action. Our mission is to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and mindset needed to create value in a fast-paced and ever-changing global economy.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading institution in entrepreneurship education, recognized for our commitment to student success, innovation, and societal impact.

Our Programs

We offer a range of programs designed to meet the diverse needs of our students. Whether you’re an aspiring entrepreneur or an established business owner looking to scale up, our programs provide the tools and resources necessary for success.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expert Faculty: Learn from a team of experienced entrepreneurs and industry professionals who bring real-world insights into the classroom.
  • Innovative Curriculum: Our curriculum combines theory with practical application, ensuring students are well-prepared for the challenges of the entrepreneurial journey.
  • Strong Network: Join a community of like-minded individuals and gain access to a wide network of entrepreneurs, investors, and industry experts.
  • Supportive Environment: We provide a supportive environment that encourages creativity, collaboration, and risk-taking.

Join us at Oduduwa University’s School of Entrepreneurship and take the first step towards turning your entrepreneurial dreams into reality.
